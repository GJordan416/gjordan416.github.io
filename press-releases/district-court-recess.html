PRESS RELEASE REGARDING THE DISTRICT COURT INDEFINITE RECESS

The Supreme Court accepted a request for indefinite recess from the Chief Judge of the Mayflower District Court of the New Haven District. In response, the Supreme Court entered a proclamation suspending judicial operations in the mentioned district until further notice.

During this time, all cases in the New Haven District Court are suspended with a continuance until the proclamation is lifted by the Court. This order extends to expungement petitions and all general warrants as well.
